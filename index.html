Services

iHerdz Dental Clinic Services

Consultation

The doctor does a complete medical review during the session by asking questions. The concerns may address symptoms, the patient's general condition or feelings, any health concerns, and the findings of past medical testing.

Oral Prophylaxis

A dental prophylaxis is a cleaning procedure performed to thoroughly clean the teeth.

Fluoride Application

Fluoride joins the tooth structure as it develops, strengthening the enamel and making the teeth less susceptible to bacteria and cavities.

Tooth Extraction

A tooth extraction is the removal of a tooth from its socket in the gum. It is normally performed by a conventional dentist, an oral surgeon, or a periodontist.

Temporomandibular Disorder

Temporomandibular disorders (TMD) are chronic facial pain caused by abnormalities of the jaw muscles, temporomandibular joints, and nerves. TMD can be caused by any condition that hinders the complex system of muscles, bones, and joints from working in harmony.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal therapy (endodontics) is a dental operation used to treat infection in the center of a tooth. Root canal therapy is not unpleasant and can salvage a tooth that would otherwise have to be extracted totally.

Orthodontics

It is a branch of dentistry concerned with the diagnosis, prevention, management, and correction of misaligned teeth and jaws, as well as misaligned bite patterns.

Cosmetics

Cosmetic dentistry refers to any dental work that improves the appearance of the teeth, gums, and/or bite. It is largely concerned with improving dental aesthetics in terms of color, position, shape, size, alignment, and overall smile appearance.

Denture

It is a type of dental treatment that is permanently linked to adjacent teeth or dental implants to restore lost teeth.
